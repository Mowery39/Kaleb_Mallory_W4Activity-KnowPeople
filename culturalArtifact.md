# Design Artifact - Cultural Artifact of Big-Box Club Shoppers

## Common Artifacts:
- Oversized carts and flatbeds
- Bulk packaging
- Membership cards
- Long receipts
- Family and group shopping behavior

## User Personas
- Budget-Focused Family Shopper
- Small Business / Side Hustle Shopper
- Value-Driven Individual Shopper

## Jobs
- Stock up efficiently
- Save money long-term
- Buy trusted brands
- Reduce shopping frequency

## Gains
- Lower cost per unit
- One-stop shopping
- Reliable product quality

## Pains
- Crowds
- Long lines
- Membership fees
- Storage issues
